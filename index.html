Hobbies

Fishing

I love fishing, though I don’t get to do it often. My favorite memories are of me fishing in Michigan when my family would go visit their relatives there. It’s relaxing to go fishing so long as I’m not in the sun for too long.

Hiking

I don’t get why more people don’t like hiking. For me it’s barely exercise. The walks are somewhat slow, but when I find something that looks fun to climb or a trail that’s somewhat rocky that’s where the fun begins. Nothing to me is more fun than listening to music while on a semi perilous trail.

Gaming

I really like playing video games. Not only can they be pretty relaxing, but they can allow for frantic or off the wall types of fun that I really enjoy. Right now I play alot of Overwatch mainly with my friends

Reading

I try to read just a little every night. Currently I am reading the wealth of nations by Adam Smith and the art of war by Sun Tzu. It’s hard to find time to read but I make the time.

Minecraft

I like playing Minecraft a lot. I have been playing it since I was like 5 and I still play it regularly as a form of meditation. I have had a survival world that I have been solo-playing in for a long time.

Keeping Rockland Beautiful

I enjoy participating in KRB clean ups whether it be alone or with friends. My reason for enjoying them is incredibly selfish, I get to walk on dangerous trails with beautiful views and get community service hours, a win-win situation for both me and nature.
